Overview

In this class, I learned a lot about how people interact with technology and how design can completely change the way someone uses a website or system. I didn’t realize how many small details go into making something actually feel easy to use until we started breaking down layouts, accessibility, user needs, and how the brain processes information. Learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript helped me understand how to apply those ideas in actual projects, and each lab showed me how design choices would impact the final product. Overall, this class helped me see technology from the user’s point of view, not just the person creating it, and that made a huge difference in how I think about building things now.
